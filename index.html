ABOUT ME

I experience many heartbreaks, failures, repeated semesters, failed major exams, unfinished businesses, scars, and other personal grudges --and I'm only 23. Many from those times, I had a lot of "what ifs", "I thought", and "I should've". I felt lost and scattered.

But there are also many times in between those tragic heartbeat flattening moments that I found myself still pursuing and hustling to move forward --as if it's the algorithm of life. Many firsts and positive surprises came into my life and helped me build the better, stronger, and smarter version of me right now.

And here I am taking one of my steps with Zuitt as I've finally found one of the things that keeps my heart beating and pumping so fast --to become a software engineer. I'm building a better a future for myself and I'm starting it right now together with you. All of these moments, whether tragic or not, are what makes me, Me.

There's a lot of times that I thought I'm done and I'd die -and I'm only 23, but here I'm standing firm with a statement "I'm glad I loved and I'm alive" --at the age of 23.
